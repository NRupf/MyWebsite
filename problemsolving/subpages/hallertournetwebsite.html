Designing Website of Haller-Tournet using Squarespace

Initial Need
The owner of the small Tourism-Consulting-Company, my mother Barbara Haller, wanted to establish an online presence. In order for her to be able to edit content without having to learn how to code, we chose to go with Squarespace, a simple but extremely elegant solution

Process
I cannot describe the process simpler than Squarespace themselves. Their Website-Creator is basically like a Candystore with different elements, which I matched to her desired structure. As of now some content is still missing, but I am looking forward to seeing the Haller-Tournet Website go live.
