Tutoring, Substitute Teaching, and Mentorship
It is my luck and joy that learning is my profession right now. Throughout my academic journey, I have explored many different approaches, and I'm here to help you succeed in your own.

Tutoring
From elementary school up to the first two years of university, we can make progress together across a variety of subjects. I typically teach via Zoom, Skype, or wherever I happen to be working at the time. Since in-person sessions can be preferable, especially for younger students, I can also provide tutoring on-site, with a travel fee of 30.-/hour. I generally ask for access to or copies of the relevant teaching materials used in your classes. Preparation time is included in all prices, a lesson usually lasts a full hour. If you or your parents find the cost prohibitive, please still reach out! We’ll find a solution that works. I always offer the first lesson for free, so we can get to know each other and you can see if I’m the right fit for you and if you’re benefiting from the sessions.
  • Elementary, Secondary School and lower Gymnasium levels
    • Subjects: Natural Sciences, Social Sciences, German, English, Italian, others upon request
    • Cost: 35.-/lesson
  • High School
    • Subjects: Physics, Mathematics, German, English, others upon request
    • Cost: 45.-/lesson
  • University
    • Subjects: Primarily Physics and Mathematics, always upon request
    • Cost: 55.-/lesson

Substitute Teaching
To be upfront right away, I don't have formal training in school pedagogy. However, I do have experience teaching at the secondary level, along with hundreds of lessons taught in various sports, including related J+S courses. I am open to inquiries for substitute teaching across different formats, with subjects similarly to those mentioned above. Together, we can keep your class moving forward even during your absence. I look forward to your contact!
